Noodles

  • Noodles
  • Water
  • Masala powders
  • Small amount of oil

Steps

  1. Take a pan or whatever which can able to make noodles
  2. boil some water in it
  3. if the bubbles started popup, put the noodles in it
  4. mix well and add our noodles masala in it
  5. wait until the water almost dissapear
  6. and serve it on a bowl with a chopstick :)
